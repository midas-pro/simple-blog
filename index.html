Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle communication and updates?

I work with US and EU clients daily, so I'm fluent in English and timezone-flexible. You'll get regular updates via Slack, email, or your preferred tool. For larger projects, we'll have weekly calls to review progress and align on next steps.

What's the typical timeline for an MVP?

Most MVPs take 4-8 weeks depending on scope. Simple products (auth, database, core features) are on the shorter end. More complex projects with AI integrations, payment systems, or Web3 components may take longer. I'll give you a clear timeline after our discovery call.

Who owns the code and IP?

You do. 100%. All code, designs, and intellectual property belong to you upon project completion and final payment. You'll get full access to repositories and documentation.

How do you handle security and sensitive data?

Security is non-negotiable. I follow industry best practices: secure authentication (JWT, OAuth), encrypted data storage, environment variable management, regular security audits, and compliance with GDPR/CCPA when needed. I can sign NDAs and work under your security protocols.

What if I'm in a different timezone?

I work with US East Coast, West Coast, and European clients regularly. I'm flexible with meeting times and maintain async communication for smooth collaboration across timezones.

What are your payment terms?

For MVPs: typically 50% upfront, 50% on completion. For retainers: monthly invoicing with net-15 terms. All payments via wire transfer, Stripe, or platform escrow (Upwork, Base44). Flexible terms available for long-term partnerships.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. I've worked with in-house teams, other contractors, and agencies. I'm comfortable with Git workflows, code reviews, and team collaboration tools. I can lead development or integrate seamlessly into your existing process.
